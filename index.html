Our Story

A Historic Landmark
Since 1908

Hotel Santo Tomás is a registered Historic Landmark, a lovingly restored 1908 colonial mansion in the vibrant Barrio Amón neighborhood. For over 35 years and nearly one million guests, Thomas, Heidy, and our dedicated staff have made travelers feel genuinely at home.

  • Antique King Louis XV reproduction furniture in every room
  • 15-foot hand-cast Spanish plaster decorative ceilings
  • Handmade mosaic tile and hardwood floors throughout
  • Top-10 TripAdvisor ranking among 85+ San José hotels for 15+ years
  • 8-minute walk to the National Theatre of Costa Rica
