Hotel Amenities

Everything you need for an unforgettable stay: pool, jacuzzi, gym, free breakfast, 21 tours, and more. All included with no hidden extras.

Pool & Volcano Jacuzzi

Unwind in our beautiful outdoor pool featuring a playful dolphin motif, surrounded by lush tropical gardens. Our signature volcano-shaped heated jacuzzi connects to the pool via an exciting slide, a guest favorite.

  • Outdoor pool with dolphin mosaic floor design
  • Volcano-shaped heated jacuzzi with waterslide
  • Surrounded by beautiful tropical garden
  • Poolside seating and lounge areas
Open daily 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Free Hot Breakfast Daily

Start every day right with a fresh, hot Costa Rican breakfast served poolside in our beautiful tropical garden setting. Heidy's homemade hot sauces are legendary among our guests.

  • Farm eggs cooked to your order
  • Fresh tropical fruit plate
  • Gallo Pinto (traditional Costa Rican beans & rice)
  • Freshly squeezed fruit juices, made fresh daily
  • Toast, butter, jams, cereal, milk, coffee & tea
  • Heidy's famous homemade hot sauces

Open-Air Fitness Center

Work out in one of San José's most scenic gyms, a charming open-air space with arched colonial windows, Costa Rica wildlife art on the walls, and a direct view of our pool and tropical gardens. Cardio machines, a full free-weight rack, cable stations, and Cable TV make it easy to stay in your routine while on the road.

  • Treadmill
  • 2 spinning bikes
  • Stair stepper
  • Fully equipped free weights
  • Pulley & cable exercise stations
  • Cable TV while you train
  • Pool side view
